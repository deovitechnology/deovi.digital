More About DE OVI TECHNOLOGY

DE OVI TECHNOLOGY is more than just an IT training center. We are a hub for innovation, creativity, and digital empowerment. Our mission is to provide practical skills, mentorship, and opportunities for everyone to thrive in the digital world.

  • We offer hands-on workshops and seminars on trending technologies.
  • Our center is equipped with modern computers, fast internet, and comfortable learning spaces.
  • We support community outreach, free digital literacy programs, and youth empowerment initiatives.
  • Our alumni work in top companies and run successful businesses.
  • We organize tech competitions, hackathons, and career fairs.
  • We provide internship and job placement support for our students.
